A Play in Five Acts · From the cycle A King's Promise

Belmont Road

May 2026 · Five acts, complete · Documentary verse

A first-year student walks home through Southampton on a cold December night and does not arrive. Belmont Road is the play of what a country built on what happened next — the screen and the state and the law of what may be said; the towns; the coast; and a father on a cold pitch who never once mistook his son for an argument. Serialized on X in five parts; the complete play is here.

The Davos Scene

May 20, 2026 · Act V · Excerpt

January 2026, the World Economic Forum. A man has been answering panel questions for twenty-four minutes. Then he asks if he may say something — and the play does the one thing a play can do that history cannot: it dims the real hall, and lets him step out of the panel and speak directly to the audience.
